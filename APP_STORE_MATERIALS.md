## Description (4000 characters max)

Creative Drawing is the ultimate digital canvas designed especially for kids! Whether your child loves doodling, creating art, or just having fun with colors, this app makes drawing magical.

**DRAW WITH FUN BRUSHES**
Choose from 7 unique brush types that feel just like the real thing:
- Pencil - for detailed sketches and fine lines
- Marker - bold, smooth strokes with vibrant colors
- Crayon - textured strokes that feel hand-drawn
- Sparkle - add magical shimmer to your art
- Rainbow - paint with all the colors at once
- Spray - create cool airbrushed effects
- Eraser - fix mistakes easily

**ADD STICKERS & STAMPS**
Decorate your drawings with 40+ adorable stickers! Choose from:
- Animals: cats, dogs, butterflies, fish & more
- Nature: sun, moon, stars, trees & rainbows
- Fun: smiley faces, balloons, crowns & party poppers
- Shapes: hearts, stars, circles & diamonds
- Transport: cars, planes & bicycles

**CREATE CUSTOM STICKERS**
Turn any photo into a sticker! Our smart AI technology automatically removes backgrounds to create perfect custom stickers from your camera or photo library.

**SYMMETRY DRAWING**
Create beautiful patterns with symmetry modes:
- Mirror Mode - draw on both sides at once
- Flip Mode - create top and bottom reflections
- Kaleidoscope - make 4-way magical patterns

**WATCH YOUR ART COME ALIVE**
The coolest feature for young artists! Play back your drawing as an animation and watch every stroke appear just like magic. Choose timelapse or trace mode to see your creation unfold.

**SHARE EVERYWHERE**
Export your animated drawings as:
- MP4 videos
- GIFs for iMessage
- Instagram Stories format
- TikTok-ready videos

**DESIGNED FOR KIDS**
- Clean, colorful interface with big buttons
- Works on both iPhone and iPad
- Dark mode / Chalkboard style available
- Shake to start over
- 50 levels of undo/redo
- Auto-saves your work
- No ads or in-app purchases

**BEAUTIFUL BACKGROUNDS**
Choose from multiple canvas colors including chalkboard mode for a unique drawing experience.

**GALLERY**
Save unlimited drawings to your personal gallery. Open old drawings anytime to continue your masterpiece or watch the animation replay.

Creative Drawing is perfect for:
- Preschoolers learning to draw
- Kids who love art and creativity
- Rainy day entertainment
- Screen-free creative time (well, almost!)
- Making personalized cards and gifts

Download Creative Drawing today and unleash your child's creativity!
